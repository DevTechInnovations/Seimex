About us

Scientific Equipment Importers and Exporters (SEIMEX)

Scientific Equipment Importers and Exporters (SEIMEX) specializes in the import and export of scientific equipment and a select range of medical supplies.

As a trusted partner in the scientific and medical industries, SEIMEX has established itself as a reliable and innovative distributor within South Africa and beyond.

Your trusted solution for scientific equipment

SEIMEX serves as the official distributor for Hansatech, PP Systems, and Medinox, ensuring quality products and excellent service in South Africa and international markets.

The company is authorized to present quotations, participate in tenders, and provide administrative support for these partners, including training, maintenance and installation services.
