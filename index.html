About us

Trusted Scientific Equipment

Scientific Equipment Importers and Exporters (SEIMEX) is a leading distributor specializing in the import and export of high-quality scientific equipment and medical supplies. As the official distributor for renowned brands such as Hansatech, PP Systems, and Medinox, SEIMEX ensures the availability of top-tier products and exceptional service in South Africa and international markets. The company is not only authorized to provide quotations and participate in tenders but also offers comprehensive administrative support to its partners. SEIMEX’s services extend to training, maintenance, and installation, ensuring clients receive complete, ongoing support. With a reputation for innovation and reliability, SEIMEX has earned the trust of both the scientific and medical industries, solidifying its position as a reliable partner for customers both locally and abroad.
